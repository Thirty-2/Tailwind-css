    • Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the Web Dev School?
    The Web Dev School is an online school that teaches web development skills to students of all levels. Our courses cover a range of topics, from HTML and CSS to JavaScript and React.
    What are the benefits of attending the Web Dev School?
    By attending the Web Dev School, you'll gain the skills and knowledge you need to succeed in the web development industry. Our courses are designed to be hands-on and practical, so you'll be able to apply what you learn to real-world projects.
    How long does it take to complete a course?
    The length of our courses varies, but most can be completed in a few weeks to a few months. We offer flexible scheduling options, so you can learn at your own pace.

    Course-Related Questions

    What courses do you offer?
    We offer a range of courses, including HTML and CSS, JavaScript, React, and more. Check out our course catalog for a full list of offerings.
    How do I know which course is right for me?
    We offer a course advisor tool that can help you determine which course is best for your skill level and goals. You can also contact our support team for guidance.
    Can I take multiple courses at once?
    Yes, you can take multiple courses at once. However, we recommend taking one course at a time to ensure you can focus on the material and get the most out of your learning experience.

    Payment and Enrollment Questions

    How much do courses cost?
    Our course prices vary, but most courses cost ranges from ₦ 50,000 to ₦ 300,000. We offer discounts for bundle purchases and loyalty rewards for repeat customers.
    How do I enroll in a course?
    To enroll in a course, simply click the "Enroll" button on the course page and follow the prompts to complete your registration. You'll need to create an account and provide payment information to secure your spot.
    Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with a course?
    Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with a course, you can request a refund within 30 days of enrollment.

    Support and Resources Questions

    What kind of support do you offer?
    We offer a range of support options, including email support, live chat, and phone support. We also have a community forum where you can connect with other students and instructors.
    Do you offer any additional resources?
    Yes, we offer a range of additional resources, including video tutorials, coding challenges, and project templates. We also have a blog that features industry news and tips.
